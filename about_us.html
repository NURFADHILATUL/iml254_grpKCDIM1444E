OUR STORY



Oui Oui Cafe was born from the French words that inspired us to open the cafe.
As for the reason we opened the cafe, it was because we love sweets so much that we decided to create our cafe.
Oui Oui Cafe is a cafe that was founded by Malaysian entrepreneurs which is us, Hanis, Akma, Ayuni, and Aliah.
True to the founder's identity, the cafe aspires to let the customers feel comfortable with our cafe environment.



VISION


To become the most beloved cafe that offers an exquisite yet affordable meal for patrons.




MISION


Oui Oui cafe strives to offer a cozy and welcoming environment where patrons can savor
our affordable meals or even quench their thirst with our high-quality beverages.
