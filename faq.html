❔Frequently Asked Questions❔

Is Oui Oui Cafe a halal cafe?

Yes, our cafe is 100% halal. We only use halal-sourced ingredients.

What are your opening hours?

Oui Oui Cafe is open 9am to 6pm on weekdays and 12pm to 9pm on weekends. We are closed on Wednesdays and public holidays.

Do we need to make a reservation?

No, you don't have to make a reservation. However, we accept reservations. You can make a reservation through our website, by calling us, or by visiting us in person.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments.

How much is the shipping fee?

The shipping fee varies depending on your location and the size of your order. Please refer to our shipping policy for more details.

Is there parking available?

Of course! We have a parking lot available for our customers.

Are pets allowed?

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed inside Oui Oui Cafe. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.
